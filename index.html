who am i

Let me introduce myself. I’m Stanislav Kukarkin. I’m 26 y.o. I live in Moscow. As for my childhood, I studied in linguistic Gymnasium and there I learned 2 languages: English and German. I speak English well enough, but my German knowledges are not so good. Well, then I passed my state exams, and entered at Tumen’ gas and oil university, I liked being there. The program was interesting and I worked as a driller assistant and as an engineer.

When I was a driller assistant, I did chute-lift operations, I controlled filtration of drilling mud. As an engineer, I controlled indicators of drilling mud and I prepared recipes for it. Then I served the army (in artilerity troops). After I started to work at the brewery, Sun inbew Efes. Then I returned to the drilling sphere but not for a long period because it`s not my cup of tea.

I realized that I want to work in IT. Despite the fact I haven`t huge experience, I`m too much interested in IT that is why my skills develop very quickly.
